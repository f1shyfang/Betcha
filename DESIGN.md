# Design System - Betcha

## Product Context
- **What this is:** Betcha is a social prediction-market web app where friends can create markets on anything, keep each other accountable, and make the whole thing feel fun instead of clinical.
- **Who it's for:** Small friend groups, curious builders, and people who want a lightweight way to make bets, track outcomes, and joke around with structure.
- **Space/industry:** Social betting and prediction markets.
- **Project type:** Web app.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Direction:** Playful-Modern.
- **Decoration level:** Intentional.
- **Mood:** Energetic, social, fast, and lightly irreverent. It should feel like a sharp product with personality, not a finance dashboard that forgot how to smile.
- **Reference sites:** Manifold, Metaculus, PredictIt.

## Typography
- **Display/Hero:** Satoshi or Cabinet Grotesk - friendly, bold, and modern for big product statements.
- **Body:** Source Sans 3 - neutral and readable for market descriptions, activity, and supporting copy.
- **UI/Labels:** Same as body.
- **Data/Tables:** Geist or DM Sans with tabular-nums - keeps prices, odds, and counts aligned.
- **Code:** JetBrains Mono.
- **Loading:** Use Google Fonts or Bunny Fonts for quick delivery; prefer self-hosting later if performance work needs it.
- **Scale:** 14px, 16px, 20px, 28px, 40px with rem-based steps.

## Color
- **Approach:** Balanced.
- **Primary:** #FF5A5F - main action color and the brand's energetic voice.
- **Secondary:** #00C2A8 - social and success accents.
- **Support:** #FFB86B - warm highlight for special states and prompts.
- **Neutrals:** #FAFBFB, #F2F4F6, #E6E9EB, #C7CDD1, #121417.
- **Semantic:** success #00C2A8, warning #FFB86B, error #E84D4D, info #4D9AFE.
- **Dark mode:** Keep surfaces dark and calm, reduce accent saturation about 15-20%, and preserve contrast on odds and CTA text.

## Spacing
- **Base unit:** 8px.
- **Density:** Comfortable.
- **Scale:** 2xs(2) xs(4) sm(8) md(16) lg(24) xl(32) 2xl(48) 3xl(64).

## Layout
- **Approach:** Hybrid.
- **Grid:** 12-column on desktop, 6-column on tablet, single column on mobile.
- **Max content width:** 1100px.
- **Border radius:** sm 6px, md 12px, lg 20px, full 9999px.

## Motion
- **Approach:** Minimal-functional with playful emphasis where it matters.
- **Easing:** enter ease-out, exit ease-in, move ease-in-out.
- **Duration:** micro 60-100ms, short 150-250ms, medium 250-400ms, long 400-700ms.
